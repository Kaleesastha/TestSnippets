Fixing WebNMS Vulnerabilities

WebNMS is fixed all the vulnerabilities in the below vulnerability reports.

https://packetstormsecurity.com/files/138244/WebNMS-Framework-5.2-SP1-Traversal-Weak-Obfuscation-User-Impersonation.html
http://seclists.org/fulldisclosure/2016/Aug/54
https://www.exploit-db.com/exploits/40229/

Here are the steps to be followed:

(Note that each link is a document. It may require a day or two to complete all steps properly.
We'd verified this and it worked for us till the client login - a sample build is also available for your reference.
Please ensure that the steps are carried as per the document)

1. Sign all the jars in WebNMS (if not already done)

2. Ensure to authenticate all requests. Please check this page as how to do it.

3. Download GetChallengeServlet.java & FileUploadServlet.java under NMS_HOME/servlets and compile them under WEB-INF/classes.

4. Disable FetchFile servlet by disabling the below 2 entries in WEB-INF/web.xml  
link1 link2  (Corresponding comment those entries in web-header/web-footer files too)

5. Encrypt WebNMS passwords with SHA-1 encryption

Once the above steps are done and restarted, WebNMS will be free from the vulnerabilities.

P.S. If you are using WebNMS java client, please contact nms-support@webnms.com
